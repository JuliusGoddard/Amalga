Multidisciplinary Innovation

Amalga builds collaborative consortia on both an IP-sharing and consultancy contract basis. We also have extensive experience in the preparation and submission of successful grant proposals to a variety of funding bodies including Innovate-UK/SBRI, The Wellcome Trust and The Gates Foundation.

What we do

Amalga Technologies Ltd collaborates with industry and academia to develop novel products and processes for the life sciences. Our speciality is multidisciplinary innovation. The company's current portfolio includes investigative projects in convolutional neural networks for the analysis of Raman spectra, cyclic peptides, electrochemical separation, fermentation, molecular modelling, multi-functionalised microalgae for the improvement of feed conversion efficiency and paediatric medical devices.
